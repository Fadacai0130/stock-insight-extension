# Privacy Policy for Stock Insight AI Chrome Extension

**Last Updated:** December 4, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Stock Insight AI Chrome Extension ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data We Do NOT Collect
We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Browsing history
- Financial account information or credentials
- Payment information

### Data Stored Locally
- **User Preferences:** Settings and configurations you choose within the Extension are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

### Data Transmitted to Third-Party Services
When you use the Extension to view stock insights, the following data may be transmitted to third-party services:
- **Stock Symbols:** The stock ticker symbols you choose to analyze are sent to external APIs to fetch market data and generate insights.

## How We Use Your Information

- Stock symbols are used solely to fetch relevant stock data and generate AI-powered insights
- User preferences are stored locally to maintain your settings between sessions
- We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the Extension's core functionality

## Data Storage and Security

- All user preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- We do not store your data on our servers
- Stock data requests are made directly to third-party APIs

## Third-Party Services

The Extension uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

- **Yahoo Finance API** (query1.finance.yahoo.com): Used to fetch real-time stock market data
- **SiliconFlow API** (api.siliconflow.cn): Used to generate AI-powered stock insights

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View what data the Extension stores locally
- **Delete:** Clear all Extension data by removing the Extension from your browser
- **Control:** Modify your preferences at any time through the Extension's settings

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Consent

By using the Stock Insight AI Chrome Extension, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
